# Runic Dice

<ItemImage id="iska_utils:runic_dice" />

## What it does

**Charm artifact.** It creates strange optical illusions — sometimes you see it split into copies and merge again. While worn, it grants a **random attack speed bonus** that shifts over time.

Wear in the **Curios charm slot**.

## How to obtain

From **Suspicious Delivery**.
