# Busted Crown

<ItemImage id="iska_utils:busted_crown" />

## What it does

**Cursed artifact.** No matter who it belonged to, it is made of plastic. Worn in **Curios**, it grants **extra maximum health** for each other **cursed artifact** you wear. The **Cursed Candle** counts even if you only carry it in your **inventory or hotbar** (not only in Curios).

## How to obtain

Often from **Suspicious Delivery**.
