# The Deception

<ItemImage id="iska_utils:the_deception" />

## What it does

**Cursed artifact.** You feel confused: sit down or eat? When in doubt, you eat. After eating, you gain **absorption** (temporary extra hearts).

Wear in **Curios**.

## How to obtain

Often from **Suspicious Delivery**.
