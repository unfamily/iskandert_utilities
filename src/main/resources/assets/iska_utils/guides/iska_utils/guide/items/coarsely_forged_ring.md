# Coarsely Forged Ring

<ItemImage id="iska_utils:coarsely_forged_ring" />

## What it does

A rough ring waiting for entropic refinement. On its own it has no powers — it is the **base** for smithing the **Entropic Ring**.

## How to obtain

From **Suspicious Delivery** only. Upgrade it at a **Smithing Table** with an **Entropic Smithing Template** and an **Entropy Crystal**.
