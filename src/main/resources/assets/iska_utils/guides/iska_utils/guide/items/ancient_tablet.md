# Ancient Tablet

Hold the tablet in your **main hand**. It stores up to **64** ingredients (one item per slot).

## Adding ingredients

- In your inventory, **click** another stack onto the tablet (or the tablet onto another stack), same idea as a bundle.

## Emptying

- **Right-click** in the air or on a block: drops **all** contents at your feet.

## Crafting

- Fill the tablet to match a recipe (see **JEI** → Ancient Tablet).
- **Left-click** in the air or on a block to craft.
- Some recipes require items in a **specific order**; JEI warns when that applies.

## Recycling delivery loot

Any item from a Suspicious Delivery opening (and the parcel itself), except **Drop of Chaos**, can be turned into **Drop of Chaos** in a **crafting table** or in the **Factory**.
