# Ancient Tablet

<ItemImage id="iska_utils:ancient_tablet" />

Hold the tablet in your **main hand**. It stores up to **64** ingredients (one item per slot).

## Adding ingredients

- In your inventory, **click** another stack onto the tablet (or the tablet onto another stack), same idea as a bundle.

## Emptying

- **Right-click** in the air or on a block: drops **all** contents at your feet.
- In your inventory: **right-click** the tablet to take the **last inserted** item out.

## Crafting

- Fill the tablet with the required ingredients (shown on the item tooltip when relevant).
- **Left-click** in the air or on a block to craft.
- Some crafts need items in a **specific order**; the tooltip warns when that applies.

## Outputs

The tablet forges <ItemImage id="iska_utils:entropy_crystal" /> **Entropy Crystal** and <ItemImage id="iska_utils:unstable_entropy_catalyst" /> **Unstable Entropy Catalyst**. Both need <ItemImage id="iska_utils:drop_of_entropy" /> **Drop of Entropy** — see that page for how to get drops (**Suspicious Delivery** and recycling).

## Obtaining the tablet

Most often from opening <ItemImage id="iska_utils:suspicious_delivery" /> **Suspicious Delivery**. Can also be **recycled** into drops if you already have one and do not need it.
