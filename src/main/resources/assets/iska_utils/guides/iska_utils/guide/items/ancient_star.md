# Ancient Star

<ItemImage id="iska_utils:ancient_star" />

## What it does

A relic for the Curios **curio** slot (not cursed). While equipped:

- **Always:** bonus armor
- **High HP:** bonus outgoing damage
- **Low HP:** bonus armor toughness

Only one of the combat bonuses applies at a time besides the base armor.

## How to obtain

From **Suspicious Delivery**.
