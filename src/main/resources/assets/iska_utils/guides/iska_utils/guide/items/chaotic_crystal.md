# Chaotic Crystal

<ItemImage id="iska_utils:chaotic_crystal" />

## What it is

A crystallized chaos resource forged on the **Ancient Tablet**. It is more stable than an **Unstable Drop** and does not decay in your inventory.

## How to craft

Load the tablet with the ingredients shown in **JEI** → **Ancient Tablet** (typically amethyst gems and **Drop of Chaos**). **Left-click** with the filled tablet in your main hand to craft.

Check **JEI** for exact amounts and tags in your modpack.
