# Ritual Gauntlet

<ItemImage id="iska_utils:ritual_gauntlet" />

## What it does

**Cursed artifact** covered in runes. In **Curios**, while you have a **beneficial effect** active, your critical hits can deal **extra damage**.

## How to obtain

Often from **Suspicious Delivery**.
