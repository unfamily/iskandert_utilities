# The Chosen Cheese

<ItemImage id="iska_utils:chosen_cheese" />

## What it does

After you taste it, you feel reinvigorated. Worn in **Curios**, it slowly raises your **maximum health** the longer you keep it.

**Shift + right-click** with another cheese in your inventory to merge its progress into this one (the other cheese is consumed).

## How to obtain

Often from **Suspicious Delivery**.
