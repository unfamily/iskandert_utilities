# Totem of Pain

<ItemImage id="iska_utils:totem_of_pain" />

## What it does

**Cursed artifact.** Even small cuts become bleeding wounds — your attacks can apply **Curse of Pain** on enemies.

Wear in **Curios** for the effect.

## How to obtain

Often from **Suspicious Delivery**.
