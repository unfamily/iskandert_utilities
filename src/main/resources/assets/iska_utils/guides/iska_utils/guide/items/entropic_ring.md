# Entropic Ring

<ItemImage id="iska_utils:entropic_ring" />

## What it does

A **cursed** ring for the Curios **ring** slot. While you have **less HP than your target**, your hits gain **flat bonus damage** based on the HP gap. With **Apotheosis** loaded, the bonus scales with your **world tier**.

## How to obtain

Smith a **Coarsely Forged Ring** at a **Smithing Table** (Entropic template + Entropy Crystal). The rough ring itself comes from **Suspicious Delivery**.
