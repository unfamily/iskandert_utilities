# Dye Berries

<ItemImage id="iska_utils:dye_berry" />

## Obtaining

- Harvest from **Dye Bush** blocks when they are filled (bushes refill over time).
- Plant new bushes with leaves, redstone, lapis, and **Green Sludge**.

## Uses

- Craft vanilla and Dyenamics dyes at a crafting table or **Factory** (see JEI for color mappings).
- Edible, but not very appetizing.
