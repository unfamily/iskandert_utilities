# Entropy materials

Progression built around the **Ancient Tablet**: a portable crafter that holds ingredients and performs special recipes. You need **Drop of Entropy** (usually from opening a **Suspicious Delivery**) as a key component.

From the tablet you can forge **Entropy Crystal** and **Unstable Entropy Catalyst**. The catalyst decays in your inventory; plan ahead when crafting it. **Entropy TNT** is crafted from TNT, crystals, and the catalyst.
