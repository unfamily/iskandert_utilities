# Graveyard Soil

<ItemImage id="iska_utils:graveyard_soil" />

A **companion block** for the entropic system — not entropic soil. It does not spread with agglomeration, join entropic spawn networks, or turn into entropic dirt in light.

## Behavior

Undead (`#minecraft:undead`) standing on graveyard soil are **healed** on a random interval (configurable).

## With entropic soil

Use it alongside entropic patches to support undead; see **Entropic Soil** for spread, light, and spawn mechanics.
