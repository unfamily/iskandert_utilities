# Sharpened Bone

<ItemImage id="iska_utils:sharpened_bone" />

## What it does

Said to have belonged to a god of war. In **Curios**, your melee attacks hit **harder** and sometimes **ignore armor**.

## How to obtain

Often from **Suspicious Delivery**.
