# Entropic terrain

Entropy-related ground blocks: **Entropic Soil** (darkness, hostile spawns, light decay to Entropic Dirt), **Graveyard Soil** (undead support — separate from entropic soil), and **Druidic Podzol** (light, animal spawns, no decay or dormant form).
