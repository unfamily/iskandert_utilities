# Ice Diamond

<ItemImage id="iska_utils:ice_diamond" />

## What it does

A fake gem made of ice. In **Curios**, it **repairs your tools** over time, but the diamond itself **wears out** and tends to melt away with use.

## How to obtain

Often from **Suspicious Delivery**.
