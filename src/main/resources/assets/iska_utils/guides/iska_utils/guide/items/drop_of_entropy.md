# Drop of Entropy

<ItemImage id="iska_utils:drop_of_entropy" />

## What it does

A rare **resource** relic — not worn on the body. Used in crafting and progression; check **JEI** for recipes in your pack.

## How to obtain

Most often from opening **Suspicious Delivery** (uncommon reward).
