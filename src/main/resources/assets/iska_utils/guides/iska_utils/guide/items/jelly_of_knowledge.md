# Jelly of Knowledge

<ItemImage id="iska_utils:jelly_of_knowledge" />

## Obtaining

Produced by the <ItemImage id="iska_utils:knowledge_compressor" /> **Knowledge Compressor** (under **World and machines**) when its experience fluid tank has enough stored XP.

## How to use

- **Use** (right-click) while holding a jelly to gain the stored experience points.
- **Shift + use** to consume the entire stack in one action.

Check the item tooltip for the exact XP and fluid cost per jelly (configurable).
