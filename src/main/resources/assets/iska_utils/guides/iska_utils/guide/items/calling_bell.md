# Calling Bell

<ItemImage id="iska_utils:calling_bell" />

## What it does

Arcane **charm** curio. While equipped, if you wear at least **three distinct arcane artifacts** in Curios at the same time, you gain bonus max health, armor, and armor toughness.

The count ignores the bell itself. Only arcane artifacts count — normal Curios artifacts such as Ancient Star or Old Brick do not.

## Obtaining

Often found inside <ItemImage id="iska_utils:suspicious_delivery" /> **Suspicious Delivery** parcels.

## Tips

- Pair with arcane artifacts such as <ItemImage id="iska_utils:busted_crown" /> **Busted Crown**, <ItemImage id="iska_utils:totem_of_pain" /> **Totem of Pain**, or <ItemImage id="iska_utils:ritual_gauntlet" /> **Ritual Gauntlet** — each different arcane artifact type counts once.
