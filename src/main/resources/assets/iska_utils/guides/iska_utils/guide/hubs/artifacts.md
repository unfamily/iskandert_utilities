# Suspicious Delivery

<ItemImage id="iska_utils:suspicious_delivery" />

Relics and special curios obtained mainly by opening a **Suspicious Delivery** parcel.

## How to get the parcel

- **Wandering Trader** — sometimes **drops** it, or **trades** it for emeralds (see the parcel tooltip in-game).
- **Optional:** with the **Artifacts** mod, the **Mimic** can rarely drop the **parcel itself** — relics inside still come only from **opening** the parcel.

## Opening

Use the parcel like a normal item. It is **consumed** and grants **one random** outcome. **Luck** (Fortune) and **Bad Luck** (Misfortune) influence rarer rewards while opening.

## What is inside

Outcomes include **Drop of Entropy**, delivery **relics** (pages below), **Durable Shears**, a **nested parcel**, or harmless **joke** results (for example a clock). Each relic has its own guide page under this hub.

## Recycling

Unwanted **unopened parcels** and most **delivery relics** can be turned into **Drop of Entropy** at a crafting table or **Factory** — see **Drop of Entropy** under **Entropy materials**. **Drop of Entropy** itself is **not** recyclable.

## Curios

Most relics are worn in **Curios** slots. **Cursed Candle** is an exception and works from your hotbar or inventory.

**Entropy materials** (tablet, crystals, entropic gear) are listed in the next hub below.
