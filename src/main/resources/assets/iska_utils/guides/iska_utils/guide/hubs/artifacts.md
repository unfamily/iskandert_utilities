# Suspicious Delivery

<ItemImage id="iska_utils:suspicious_delivery" />

Artifacts and special curios obtained mainly by opening **Suspicious Delivery**.

## How to obtain

- **Wandering Trader** — sometimes **drops** it, or **trades** it for emeralds (see the **Suspicious Delivery** tooltip in-game).
- **Optional:** with the **Artifacts** mod, the **Mimic** can rarely drop **Suspicious Delivery** itself — artifacts inside still come only from opening one.

## Opening

Use it like a normal item. It is **consumed** and grants **one random** outcome. The vanilla **Luck** and **Bad Luck** effects influence rarer rewards while opening.

## What is inside

Outcomes include **Drop of Entropy**, delivery **artifacts** (pages below), **Durable Shears**, another **Suspicious Delivery**, or harmless **joke** results (for example a clock). Each artifact has its own guide page under this hub.

## Recycling

Unwanted unopened **Suspicious Delivery** and most **delivery artifacts** can be turned into **Drop of Entropy** at a crafting table or **Factory** — see **Drop of Entropy** under **Entropy materials**. **Drop of Entropy** itself is **not** recyclable.

## Curios

Most artifacts are worn in **Curios** slots. **Cursed Candle** is an exception and works from your hotbar or inventory.

**Entropy materials** (tablet, crystals, entropic gear) are listed in the next hub below.
