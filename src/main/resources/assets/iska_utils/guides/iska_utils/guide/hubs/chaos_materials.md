# Chaos materials

Progression built around the **Ancient Tablet**: a portable crafter that holds ingredients and performs special recipes. You need **Drop of Chaos** (usually from opening a **Suspicious Delivery**) as a key component.

From the tablet you can forge **Chaotic Crystal** and **Unstable Drop**. The unstable drop decays in your inventory; plan ahead when crafting it.
